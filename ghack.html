Recycling Process

The e-waste recycling process involves the following steps:

  1. Collection and Transportation: E-waste is collected and sent to recycling centers.
  2. Sorting and Dismantling: Devices are manually sorted and dismantled.
  3. Shredding and Separation: Components are shredded and metals are separated using advanced technologies.
  4. Metal Extraction: Precious metals like gold, silver, and copper are extracted using hydrometallurgical and pyrometallurgical processes.
